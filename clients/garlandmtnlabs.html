Garland Mtn Labs

Website Proposal

Design

Intro:
The format of the website will be a single page where users scroll to get to each section. Everything here can be changed, these are just my suggestions for the design; especially the colors that I arbitrarily chose for each section. When the website gets made it will also look a lot cleaner and everything will be more lined up than my photoshop skills allow me to illustrate in the mock up.

I also do photography on the side and would be happy to take any pictures for you that are needed for the initial setup of the website. You can find my photography work HERE.

Walkthrough:
The navbar (labeled A) will stay at the top of the screen no matter how far the user scrolls down the page. It will also have clickable headers that automatically scroll the user to the clicked section.

At the top of the page above the ‘Our Pack’ section, will be a picture of your choice showing your dog(s).

The ‘Our Pack’ section (labeled B) will have text that provides general information about your breeding dogs. It will also have rotating cards that will have a picture of each dog with a section for text that is specific to each dog.

The ‘Our Puppies’ section (labeled C) will have text that provides general information about the current puppies that you have. It also features a photo gallery that rotates between the male puppies of the litter and the female puppies. When you currently do not have any puppies for sale, we can change the text to let users know when your next planned litter will be.

The ‘About Us’ section (labeled D) will contain text about Garland Mtn Labs and its history, etc. It will also feature a picture that can be of the dogs, your family that helps with the dogs, etc.

The last section at the bottom (labeled E) will have your contact information so users can reach out to you.
